Scientific-Calculator

A basic scientific calculator with limited functions such as addition (+), subtraction (–), multiplication (×), division (÷), percentage (%), and reciprocal (1/x) is designed to perform essential mathematical calculations with accuracy and speed. Even though it does not include advanced functions like trigonometry or logarithms, it is highly useful for everyday mathematical tasks, school-level calculations, and general problem-solving. The addition and subtraction keys allow users to perform simple arithmetic operations. Multiplication and division help in solving numeric expressions, ratios, and scaling problems. The percentage (%) function is particularly helpful for calculating discounts, interest, tax, profit–loss, and comparisons. The reciprocal key (1/x) allows the user to quickly find the inverse of a number, which is useful in fraction calculations, division shortcuts, and solving basic algebraic expressions. Such calculators usually include a clear (C) button to reset the display, ensuring error-free calculations. They often have a simple, easy-to-read screen and responsive keys that make them user-friendly. Although limited compared to full scientific calculators, they are efficient, reliable, and ideal for students and users who need fast arithmetic and percentage-based calculations in daily life.
